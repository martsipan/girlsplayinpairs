About the project Diary Participants

Welcome to the Girls Play in Pairs (Flickor leker två och två) project! This is an interdisciplinary research project that explores various ways of making and presenting art. The research is conducted through the model of affidamento (entrustment) which is a supportive pair-relationship developed by Italian feminist groups in the 1960s and 70s.

Flickor leker två och två is supported by Kulturbryggan.
