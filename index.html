About the project Diary Participants

Girls Play in Pairs (Flickor leker två-och-två) is an interdisciplinary research project that explores various ways of making and presenting art. The research is conducted through the model of affidamento (entrustment) a supportive pair-relationship developed by Italian feminist groups in the 1960’s and -70’s.

Flickor leker två-och-två is supported by Kulturbryggan.
